Biography

Hello everyone, my name is Millicent Nwokorie but i prefer being called Miley My biography spans from the indigenous peple of Umuodagu Nsu in Ehime Mbano community of the Igbo land.
The soil is know for its red color and sticky nature. Often times, the roads get slippery when it rains due to the muddy nature of the soil.
My ancestry dates back to the prehistoric times when plantain leaves were used as for the body. My forefather went to the farm to pick some bush fruits when he encountered an animal of a deathly nature.
It was a black jaguar. It stared at my forefather who could make no noise else his life could be taken away from him in seconds. My forefather being the friendly man that he was stared back into the jaguar's eyes for what seemed like minutes but was seconds.
After which, it seemed like the understood eachother because that animal became his spirit animal. He became known as the black jaguar and that animal became our family crest.
